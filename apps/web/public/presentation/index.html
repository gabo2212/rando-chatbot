06 / WHAT IT DOESUSER FLOW

The actual point

What RANDO does for you

Drop in your docs, ask questions, get answers grounded in your own files — then mess around with the games when your brain is fried.

1

Sign in

Email + password via Better Auth. Your chats and uploads stay private to your session.

2

Upload knowledge

Drop PDFs, DOCX, text, or code. RANDO chunks and embeds them into pgvector so it can cite them later.

3

Chat with your files

Ask anything. Streaming answers pull the most relevant chunks and stay grounded in what you gave it — no hallucinated sources.

4

Play & embed

Run the in-chat escape room, hop into Endless Drive, or drop the widget into any site via /embed.

Extra surfaces

Games inside the protocol

THE LAST TERMINAL

In-chat escape room narrated by A.R.I.A. Deterministic engine, persisted runs, slash commands, and AI narration over a sealed facility puzzle.

ENDLESS DRIVE

Full-bleed iframe racer at /drive — procedural low-poly world, nitro, power-slide drift, night headlights, seed worlds (DRIVE-2049).

Built as a standalone Three.js (r185) WebGL page in public/games/, embedded via iframe on /drive. Chunked terrain from a string seed hash, custom sky shader, day/night cycle, Web Audio engine loops — no React inside the game loop.
